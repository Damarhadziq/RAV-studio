Casa Luma Residence

Residential Architecture - Private House

Bali, Indonesia

End at 14 Februari 2005

“

RAV Studio brought our dream home to life — elegant, thoughtful, and truly ours.

— Alena Rivera, Owner Casa Luma Residence

Design in Words

Nestled in the heart of Bali, Casa Luma Residence stands as the first milestone in our architectural journey — a private home envisioned for a young entrepreneur named Alena Rivera, who sought a tranquil retreat away from the urban rush. Completed on February 14th, 2025, the project represents more than just 320 square meters of structure — it embodies an intimate dialogue between function, emotion, and tropical elegance.

As our debut project in residential architecture, Casa Luma became the canvas where we explored the balance of light, space, and privacy. From the open courtyards that invite Bali's morning sun to the fluid indoor-outdoor transitions that blur boundaries, every detail was crafted to echo serenity and simplicity.

What began as a modest design brief turned into a deeply personal collaboration. Casa Luma isn't just a residence — it's a reflection of intention, a space where life slows down, and design breathes.
